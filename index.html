Our Story

More than a
hair salon

Tucked into the heart of Galway City at Rockfort House, Image Hair Studio is a sanctuary where skilled artistry meets genuine warmth. Our team — led by Colin and the wonderful Natasha — are dedicated to making each visit an experience, not just an appointment.

We use professional Sebastian and Wella products, and pride ourselves on listening to exactly what you want and delivering results that consistently earn compliments.
